# 类型转换

int: 浮点型或合适的字符串(只能是整数字符串)
float: 整型或合适的字符串(只能是纯数字字符串)
str: 
```py
int('2') #2
int(2.14) #2 
int('2.14') #SyntaxError: invalid character in identifier
int(a) #SyntaxError: invalid character in identifier

float(2) #2.0
float('2.14') #2.14

str(2.14) #'2.14'
```
